The real enemy is decision fatigue

        

          Most people don’t fail because they lack discipline. They fail because dinner shows up every day,
          and every day requires decisions. This removes the decisions.
        


        
Defaults beat customization

        

          “Flexibility” sounds nice, but it hides work. The default plan is the product.
          Swaps exist only as a pressure valve if you get bored.
        


        
Simple is not “less food.” Simple is “less thinking.”

        

          Same meals. Same list. Same routines. You don’t need novelty—your brain thinks you do.
